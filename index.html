My Experiences

November 19th, 2022

In the summer before my freshman year at UMD, I took an intro programming course. I had never programmed before. I thought starting in C++ would be a good idea. To be fair, I did not know anything about programming and the content we did cover was pretty basic content. We stopped right before pointers, which I would not touch until my summer before my sophomore year. I decided CS was something I wanted to learn more about. I thought that limitless possibilities in CS is something to consider. So I decided to take a few more intro classes. I am learning Java and object oriented principles. I kept liking it more and more as I kept taking courses. I took Discrete Structures, Introduction to Computer Systems, learned C, learned Python, and learned Ruby. One after another, I kept discovering more and more. However, I feel the more I know, the less I know. Now I am at a point where I want to find an internship. So let's see how that search will go.
